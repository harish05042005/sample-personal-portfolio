About Me

An inventor, innovator, and problem-solver!

As the CEO of Stark Industries, I’ve spent my life redefining technology. From cutting-edge weapons systems to clean energy innovations, I’ve built the future with my own two hands (and a few arc reactors). I turned a life-threatening situation into a legacy of armor and AI, proving that intelligence and heart (quite literally) can change the world. ... With a mind for mechanics, a passion for progress, and a tendency to do things my way, I’ve gone from building circuits in a cave… to building hope on a global scale.
